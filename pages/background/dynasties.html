Dynasties

This section lists the various periods in the history of Beryllusir, how long they lasted and what effect they had on the world.


Era of the Gods' Rule / the Creation

Timescale unknown, thought to have lasted around 20,000 years ending in -2712. This was the period when the gods of Avalon worked on shaping the world and everything in it.


Orogai Dynasty

Began [-2712] Ended [0] Duration [2712 years]

The Orogai were the favoured species of their creator, the god Dansa. During this period they constructed a number of impressive castles and fortifications including Tinstren Castle. They also built mines to take energy from the Metrite for Ulat abilities. The Orogai Dynasty ended when the humans killed their leader, Csozhia, and wiped out most of their forces.


Black Dynasty / 17 Domains Period

Began [0] Ended [891] Duration [891 years]

During this era Beryllus was establishing her human-based society after conquering the Orogai. At the time it was called the 17 Domains Period because of the 17 main generals who each split off from her to build their own countries and empires over the next 900 years. It was later referred to by survivors and documents as the Black Dynasty due to Beryllus' craving for power and world domination. She was assassinated by her own people because of her obsession.


Feudal World Period

Began [891] Ended [present] Duration [3053+ years]

After the death of Beryllus, although it was desired by the people, the 17 Domains of Beryllusir lost their sense of unity and as a result fell into a massive state of anarchy and regression. Wars and battles errupted almost worldwide as leaders simply failed to control their countries. If this terrible event had not occured the human society would probably be MUCH more advanced today. However, after a few decades of fighting the 17 Domains for some reason decided to calm their fury and Beryllusir slowly began to build itself back to how it had been under Beryllus. This process took approximately 1,000 years for serious damage had been dealt. However, even after this shakey truce, the world still was not at peace.
