Legacies

This section lists the most famous and influential families and cults in Beryllusir.


Amun'Rah Clan

This is a cult following of the God of Life and Death - Dansa. They are somewhat fanatical in their beliefs and will go to any lengths to fulfill Dansa's wish. The clan was previously lead by Priestess Gioda until she was assassinated and Vugen took over. Kazuki was also once a member of the clan until she was banished for what was considered blasphemy. The Amun'Rah Clan finally is obliterated in the battle at Marael City during the Judgment War.


Assassin's Guild

A very mysterious organisation based in Veramonde. Due to the illegal nature of this guild they are very secretive and very few people know the exact location of its headquarters. The guild is run by Franka and has employed Sazaku and Gimora amongst other assassins. There are rumours that the guild is funded by some corrupt Harbrynd officials.


Weapons Guild

This is a guild based in north-western Veramonde which is run by the dwarven blacksmith Urgak. They construct various types of weapons for armies and organisations for a small fee. It is said that Kaiser Argane's army was planning to shut down the guild for making muskets for the Liberation Army. However, Argane was defeated before they could do this and the guild remains operational.


Destatte Family

This family is at the throne of Veramonde and the head of the family is entitled the kaiser. Kaiser Tuda was one of the most loved rulers of all time and helped establish Veramonde into the prosperous and powerful nation it is today. However he was assassinated during the war against Meluvet and his son, Granstem, assumed command. Granstem was a sick and twisted man and raped many girls working in his farmlands, then killed the baby if it was not a boy (a potential heir to the throne). The last known survivors of this family are Nastra and Serynia. Other members of the family include Argane and Rautzen.


Garalas Family

A peaceful family who have control over the free nation of Garalas. The collapse of their country came when Lord Odregas was killed during an attack from the Rebellion. Other members of the family include Martha, Kershe and Ridman.


Meluvet Family

The monarchy of the Kingdom of Meluvet. This family has a reputation for always fighting for control of vast areas of land. The first example of this is when Swalis Meluvet split Evancourt into two nations to have his own land. Secondly, Pellios Meluvet attempted to invade Veramonde but failed, and finally Agaman Meluvet tried to regain Garalas to have control of all the land that was previously Evancourt. Other members of the family include Jiame and Kira.


Regule Family

The most famous member of this family is the great warlord Cid Regule who achieved the rank of general in both Higanasu and his native land Meluvet. He holds within him the true power of the Meluvetian and it is thought that somewhere down the ancestral line the two families are connected, which would explain how he inherited this undeniable power. It is said that Regule has a wife and son who left him for unknown reasons and moved to Winstram in Krisdea.


Roshgarde Family

A family of nobles who govern the village of Milamo in Higanasu. Yail Roshgarde was the first mayor of the village because he was a close friend of the king at the time; Woregon. Edda Roshgarde is responsible for ending the Higanasu Civil War through peaceful methods which ultimately resulted in his own 'death'. His wife was Dadelia who stayed in Milamo after being widowed. They also had two sons named Kima and Eijin. Second to this, Dadelia adopted Lisa after she was orphaned by the war and allowed her to live with them.
